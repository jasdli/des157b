The UC Davis Plant-Based Guide

The food guide listed possible vegan restaurants at Davis. Vegan restaurants offer more options for people with dietary restrictions or preferences. Plant-based dishes have more health benefits compared to animal-based meals. They are often more affordable than animal-based dishes due to the lower cost of ingredients. plant-based ingredients such as vegetables, grains, and legumes are typically less expensive and more widely available. I want to include vegan restaurants from the guide to my capstone project. Vegan restaurants promote more sustainable and healthy food choices, and also provide more affordable food options.

This blog recommends restaurants that source their ingredients locally, use organic or sustainably grown produce, reduce food waste, or promote fair labor practices throughout their supply chain. This ethical restaurant guide helps people choose businesses that align with their values and preferences. Overall, it encourages more businesses to adopt ethical and sustainable practices.
