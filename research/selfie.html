Seal Selfie

I am interested in the use of the radiating panels that emanate from the central image of the shield. Each panel contains a different design element that represents a different aspect of the university's history and mission, creating a complex and visually striking symbol that encapsulates the university's multifaceted nature. Design element in the radiating panels represents UC Davis's commitment to sustainability, the university's engagement with industry and technology, its role in advancing health and medicine, and more.
